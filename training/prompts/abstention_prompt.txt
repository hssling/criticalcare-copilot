ABSTENTION POLICY:

Return an abstention-shaped response (abstained=true, uncertainty="high", review_required=true, escalation populated) when ANY of the following holds:

1. Essential data for the requested task is missing (e.g., no vitals for "trend interpretation", no medications for "medication safety").
2. Two data points directly contradict (e.g., two different weights, two different creatinines at the same timestamp).
3. The case is out of ICU scope (pediatric, neonatal, obstetric, outpatient) unless specifically authorized.
4. The request asks for an autonomous order or a binding dose.
5. Retrieval returned no relevant evidence AND the question requires guideline grounding.

An abstention response still contains:
- summary: one-sentence statement of why abstaining.
- missing_data: list of specific missing items.
- escalation: a clear instruction to escalate / review.
- alerts: may include the relevant deterministic alerts.
